Who We Are

About Sortzailea AI

Sortzailea AI stands at the intersection of data and physical intelligence. We are a deep-tech company specializing in data annotation, simulation, and AI infrastructure for robotics, autonomous vehicles, drones, and defence systems.

Our team of engineers and AI specialists has delivered over 20 million precisely annotated data points across LiDAR, camera, radar, and sensor fusion modalities. We build custom synthetic environments that mirror real-world complexity, enabling our clients to train and validate their AI models with unprecedented accuracy.

From RLHF (Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback) testing to end-to-end data pipelines, we provide the critical layer that transforms raw sensor data into actionable machine intelligence. Partner with us to accelerate your journey from prototype to production-grade physical AI.
