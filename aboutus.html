About Us

Behind The Intelligence Machine

We are Sortzailea AI — a deep-tech company powering the future of physical AI through precision data annotation, simulation, and robotics intelligence.

Our Story

From Vision to Reality

Sortzailea AI was born from a simple yet powerful belief: that the future of intelligence is physical. Founded in 2026 by a team of robotics engineers and AI researchers, we set out to solve the most critical bottleneck in autonomous systems — high-quality, scalable data.

What began as a small annotation studio has grown into a global deep-tech company serving autonomous vehicle manufacturers, drone fleets, defence organizations, and robotics startups across three continents.

Today, with over 20 million annotated data points delivered and a team of 25+ specialists, we continue to push the boundaries of what machines can perceive, understand, and act upon in the physical world.
